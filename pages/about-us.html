About us

Association "Center for Excellence in Personal Development SOLOMON" is a non-governamental and non-profit organisation who devided to get involved in educational and cultural activities in Brasov country area

It was founded on the 6th of February 2012

MISSION

Our Mission is to multiply excellence.

To make fulfill, we contribute to personal and professional development of youth, we give people the resources to develop, experiment and live their ideas, dreams, passions

This mainly through non-formal education, so that they will be able to build a career, a life that corresponds to their potential

VISION

The vision that led to this decision is following: people who have a complete culture and education are few

However, everybody else report to them.

Our vision is: those who want to become a name in society, should have access to education as complete as possible

Today we no longer believe that this is possibble only by means used so far.

VALUES

Truth, self-knowledge, wellness, honesty, competence, culture, personal development, fun, non-formal education, faimily, ethics, democracy, independence, integrity, leadership, freedom, friendship, self-esteem, responsability, health and well beeing.

FUTURE DEVELOPMENT

We constantly work on improving the activity of our organization and try to make the right choices which will make us grow.

Therefore, we focus on increasing the number of our volunteers, because we believe that human resource is what makes our NGO what it is and can be.

But we also look for expanding our international network, on developing the partnerships, in order to implement better and various activities for the youth.
